Wooden Veins, formed in 2019 by Chilean musicians mostly based in European countries, engages in classic melodic doom metal, combining it with progressive and gothic elements, exploring even further as they bring more heaviness and even Latin flavour into their sound.

"In Finitude", their debut album (the Vinyl Division Rec.), released in 2021, got an excellent response from the media, ranking the album in many lists as one of the best of 2021.

Currently the band is promoting the single "Tearing Seas", their first release after "In Finitude".
Album release and an European Tour should follow in 2023.

Wooden Veins are:

  • Alberto Atalah: Drums
  • Javier Cerda: Vox
  • Juan Escobar: Guitars
  • Juan Díaz: Bass Guitar
  • Eduardo Poblete: Synths
